Faq Questions

What safety features are included in your lifts?

Our lifts are equipped with multiple safety features, including Emergency stop buttons, Photocell full height, Door lock switch, Limit switch, Overload protection, Automatic Rescue Devices, Overspeed Governor, and regular safety inspections to ensure compliance with European safety standards (EN-81).

Can you customize the design of the lift cabin?

Yes, we offer a variety of design options for lift cabins, including materials, finishes, lighting, handrails, and also cabin, landing doors and LOP, COP, to match your building's aesthetic.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We offer 24/7 emergency support with a team of technicians ready to respond quickly to any lift breakdowns, typically within a few hours depending on your location.

How do I determine the right size generator for my needs?

Our experienced engineers can conduct a load analysis to recommend the appropriate generator size based on your power requirements, ensuring efficient and reliable backup power.

Can you install a generator in a residential area?

Yes, we provide generator installation services for both residential and commercial properties, ensuring compliance with local regulations and minimal noise disruption.

What types of generators do you offer?

We offer a wide range of generators, from the rating starts from 10 kVA to 500 kVA, suitable for different applications from small homes to large industrial facilities.

What is the warranty on your generators?

Our generators come with a manufacturer's warranty which is 1 year (12 Months). We also provide an equivalent warranty period for ATS (Automatic transfer switch).

What services do you provide for substations?

We offer a comprehensive range of substation services, including Design, Load sanction, Substation equipment installation, commissioning, maintenance, Solar Panel installation etc to ensure effective power distribution.
